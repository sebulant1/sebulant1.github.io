Games

Gooble Clicker

A simple Cookie Clicker clone made to honour the all powerful cat known as Gooble.

Also made in about 2 days, which is definitely a new record for me.

Wanted!

Wanted! is a PC and mobile web game where you must spot a hidden character hiding amongst a crowd. It's based off the old DS Mario minigame with the same name.

It was a big collaboration game with over 150 artists taking part to draw all the character sprites.

In the end this game got frontpaged, Daily 2nd, Weekly 3rd and Best of the Month. What a line-up.

Wizard & Warrior

Wizard & Warrior is a 2 player couch co-op boss rush shoot 'em up horizontal flight game jam game. In other words, you and the guy sat next to you share a keyboard and shoot a few bosses.

It was made for both Boss Rush Jam 2024 and Pixel Day 2024 over the span of around 10 days.

BYLE

BYLE is a top down rogue-like shooter. You play as a strangely cuboid shaped character roaming the streets of a city overrun by a hivemind substance known as BYLE. Pick up powerups and new weapons as enemies randomly generated levels get harder and harder.

The game was my final project for university, and I worked on it with 6 other people in the beautiful team we call ST. JARLE. We were nominated for best game of our year, but lost to the other team that had a more visually appealing game (copium).
